Ant Group has just unveiled a significant advancement in Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)!
 
Many RAG systems struggle with the complexities of multi-hop questions, often facing inefficient iterative retrieval, noisy document processing, and the accumulation of irrelevant information.
 
Their new paper, HANRAG (Heuristic Accurate Noise-resistant Retrieval-Augmented Generation), introduces a novel heuristic-based framework that intelligently tackles these challenges.
 
At its core, HANRAG features a powerful "revelator" agent that routes queries, decomposes them into precise sub-queries, and crucially, filters noise from retrieved documents.
 
This innovative approach ensures greater efficiency and robust noise resistance, delivering state-of-the-art performance across both single-hop and multi-hop question-answering tasks.
