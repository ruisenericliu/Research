Bridging Robotics and AI with Spot, Isaac Sim, and VILA  
Excited to share our latest project combining Boston Dynamics’ Spot robot, NVIDIA Isaac Sim, and the powerful VILA 1.5-3B Vision-Language Model running on Jetson AGX Orin!  
What we achieved:  
1. Simulated the Spot robot in Isaac Sim for dynamic mobility.  
Enabled Spot to understand its environment using VILA for real-time scene captions.  
2. Leveraged Jetson AGX Orin for edge AI computing to process and generate insights.  
3. This setup not only showcases Spot’s mobility but also demonstrates how advanced AI models can provide semantic understanding, empowering robots to interact meaningfully with their world.  
The combination of simulation, ROS2, and cutting-edge vision-language models is the next step toward smarter autonomous systems. Stay tuned for more innovations in robotics and AI!
